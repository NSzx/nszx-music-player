Quickstart

Quickstart

Share playlist

  1. Click on the "Share" option in the context menu (right click) for playlists.

  2. It will open a pop-up where you can rename and share a playlist.

    When clicking "Share", two sharable links will appear.

    Those links are valid for 24 hours only!

  3. The first link is a webpage where you can see the tracks you've shared. This page can be scanned as an open directory.

  4. The second link can be opened in any browser where the NSzx Media Player is installed to automatically create a new playlist in the extension.
